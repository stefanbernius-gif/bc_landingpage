## Research Findings: Why Awareness-First Makes Sense for $500-600 Budget

### SEO Gap Analysis (200-Keyword Study)

**Critical Finding:** Balam dominates branded/decision keywords but fails on awareness-stage content.

**High-volume awareness keywords where Balam underperforms:**
- "escuelas cerca de mi" → 110K monthly searches, Rank 24 (target: Top 10)
- "escuelas primarias cerca de mi" → 40.5K searches, Rank 26 (target: Top 10)
- "colegios cerca de mi" → 40.5K searches, Rank 27 (target: Top 10)
- "pet friendly Mérida Yucatán" → 18.1K searches, Rank 96 (massive gap)
- "Chuburna Puerto Yucatán" → 14.8K searches, Rank 20-25 (good but improvable)
- "haciendas en Yucatán" → 720 searches, Rank 72 (poor)

**Content type performance:**
- Proyecto/desarrollo pages: Avg Rank 19.8 (strong — no ads needed)
- Blog/guía pages: Avg Rank 36.4 (weak — ads can bridge this gap)
- Decision-stage keywords: Avg Rank 22 (strong organic)
- Awareness-stage keywords: Avg Rank 41 (weak — ads are cost-effective here)

**Geographic opportunities:**
- Mérida – Poniente: Only 1 tracked keyword (massive underserved market)
- Yucatán – General: 10 keywords, avg rank 46.3 (poor — content needs work)
- Mérida – Costa: 37 keywords, moderate performance

### Why $500-600/Month Works Better Than $3K-5K

**Original strategy assumed high-intent focus:**
- Fideicomiso, "buy property," property lawyers (low volume, high intent)
- Tight budget means fewer impressions on competitive terms
- Average CPC for "buy property Mérida" keywords: $2-5 USD

**Revised strategy uses awareness-stage keywords:**
- Schools, neighborhoods, lifestyle, pet-friendly (high volume, lower competition)
- Average CPC for awareness keywords: $0.50-1.50 USD (3-5x cheaper)
- At $600/month, you get 400-1,200 clicks instead of 120-300 clicks

**Math comparison:**
```
$3K budget on high-intent keywords:
- CPC average: $3
- Monthly clicks: 1,000
- Expected leads (3% conversion): 30
- Cost per lead: $100

$600 budget on awareness keywords:
- CPC average: $1
- Monthly clicks: 600
- Expected leads (8-10% conversion, warm traffic): 48-60
- Cost per lead: $10-12.50
```

**Plus:** Awareness traffic to blog pages improves SEO ranking → Organic traffic increases → Ad dependency decreases over 6 months.

## Phase 5: SEO Synergy (How Ads Improve Organic)

**The feedback loop:**

```
1. Run awareness ads → Blog traffic increases
   
2. Blog readers engage (1.5+ min on page, internal link clicks)
   → Google sees signals: user satisfaction, engagement time
   
3. Engagement signals improve page authority
   → Rankings improve for "escuelas," "pet friendly," "neighborhoods"
   
4. As blog pages rank better organically → Reduce ad spend
   
5. Organic traffic takes over → Lower CAC long-term
```

**Example trajectory:**
- Month 1: 500 blog visits via ads + 200 organic = 700 total
- Month 3: 600 blog visits via ads + 800 organic = 1,400 total (organic doubles)
- Month 6: 400 blog visits via ads + 2,000 organic = 2,400 total (organic takes over, ad dependency drops)

**Result:** By Month 6, same blog traffic at lower ad cost because organic has improved.

## Phase 6: Budget Justification vs. Original $3K-5K Plan

### Why Start Small ($600)?

**1. Lower-intent awareness keywords are cheaper to rank for:**
- "Escuelas cerca de mi": CPC $0.50-1.00 (vs. "buy property" at $3-5)
- 6x more clicks for same budget

**2. Blog traffic builds organic ranking:**
- Each month of ad-driven traffic improves SEO
- After 6 months, organic traffic reduces ad reliance
- $600 × 6 months = $3,600 total investment with lasting SEO benefit

**3. Risk reduction:**
- Test awareness funnel at low cost first
- Once blog pages rank better, add high-intent budget
- Proof of concept before scaling to $2K+

**4. Mexican buyer budget constraints:**
- $600/month is more defensible than $3K/month
- Shows commitment to testing, not large upfront risk
- Can scale if results prove ROI
