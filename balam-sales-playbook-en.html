📋 Lead Qualification (First 3 Minutes)

Goal: Determine if they're a qualified buyer before diving into features/benefits.

Discovery Questions (Ask These First)

1

Budget & Timeline

Ask: "What's your budget range? And how soon are you looking to close?"

Listen for: Clear budget (ideally $200K+), timeline under 6 months = hot lead

2

Buyer Type

Ask: "Is this for investment/rental, personal residence, or both?"

Listen for: Investment = higher deal value. Living = longer relationship.

3

Experience Level

Ask: "Have you bought international property before? How was that experience?"

Listen for: First-time = needs education. Experienced = expect efficiency.

4

Pain Point Validation

Ask: "What's your biggest concern about buying property in Mexico?"

Listen for: Fideicomiso fear, cost uncertainty, legal risk = perfect match for your solution

Lead Scoring

Qualification Level Criteria Next Action
🔥 HOT LEAD Budget $250K+, Timeline <6 months, Clear buyer type, Has money now Schedule showing immediately. Offer virtual tour this week.
✅ WARM LEAD Budget $150K-$250K, Timeline 3-12 months, Needs to research more Share property listings. Follow up in 1 week. Be helpful, not pushy.
❄️ COLD LEAD Budget unclear, Timeline 12+ months, Just exploring, No urgency Nurture with monthly newsletter. Stay in touch. Don't waste call time.
🚫 UNQUALIFIED Not a serious buyer, only tire-kicking, Unrealistic budget/timeline Be polite, thank them, move on. Don't spend energy on tire-kickers.

💬 Consultation Call Script (15 Minutes)

Structure: Qualification (3 min) → Education (5 min) → Objection Handling (4 min) → CTA (2 min)

Opening (0-1 minute)

YOU: "Hi [NAME], how are you? Thanks for reaching out. I see you downloaded our guide. What part interested you most?" THEM: [They respond] YOU: "Perfect. Today my goal is to understand your situation exactly and show you how we can help. Sound good?"

Discovery (1-4 minutes)

Use the 4 qualification questions above. Listen more than you talk. Take notes.

YOU: "Tell me, where were you thinking of buying? And what's the main reason you want to invest in Yucatán right now?" THEM: [They explain] YOU: "That makes sense. In the last 3 years, we've helped over 500 buyers in exactly your situation. So I know exactly what you need."

Education (4-9 minutes)

Address their specific pain point. Don't give a generic pitch — personalize to them.

If they're worried about fideicomiso:

YOU: "The good news is fideicomiso is NOT what it sounds like. It's simply legal protection for you. You're the beneficiary. A bank just acts as intermediary. And when you sell, you get everything back. It's safer than buying property in some European countries. We handle all the paperwork. You just sign."

If they're worried about costs:

YOU: "I understand. A lot of international buyers are surprised by costs they didn't expect. For a $300K property, here's the TOTAL breakdown: - Property: $300K - Commission: $15K - Fideicomiso + Legal: $5K-$9K - Title insurance + taxes: $3K-$4K - TOTAL: $323K-$328K The important thing is that this is 100% transparent BEFORE you commit. No surprises down the road."

If they're worried about timeline:

YOU: "The timeline is: Consultation → Mérida visit (2-3 days) → See properties → Sign documents → 8-10 weeks to closing. We've closed transactions in under 8 weeks and some in 12. It depends on your speed and paperwork. Do you need to rush? Or would you prefer to take it slower?"

Objection Handling (9-13 minutes)

Let them voice concerns. Don't interrupt. Then respond.

OBJECTION #1: "This sounds complicated. Do I really need an agent?"
YOUR RESPONSE: "Fair question. Technically you could do it alone. But here's the risk: a mistake in the fideicomiso, a defective title, or a badly written contract could cost you $10K-$50K in legal problems.

We have an in-house legal team that verifies EVERY title, handles the fideicomiso, and drafts contracts. It's part of the process. And honestly, it's cheaper than hiring an independent lawyer."
OBJECTION #2: "What if the property isn't worth what I paid for it?"
YOUR RESPONSE: "Good concern. That's why we select each property in neighborhoods with a track record of appreciation. In the last 5 years, north and south Mérida have appreciated 3-4% annually.

Obviously we can't guarantee appreciation (no one honestly does), but we select by location, not blind speculation. And it always helps to ask: are you buying to live in or purely to invest?"
OBJECTION #3: "Isn't there corruption? Is my money safe?"
YOUR RESPONSE: "I completely understand. Yes, there's red tape in Mexico. But no, it's not a 'gray zone.' Real estate purchases are regulated, insured, and documented with federal notaries (they're like certified public notaries).

Your money doesn't pass through our hands. It goes directly from your bank to the seller. The fideicomiso escrow protects both sides. And you get title insurance covering legal errors for up to 10 years.

We've closed 500+ transactions without a SINGLE security or fraud issue. We're NAR and AMPI certified. Reputation is everything in this business."
OBJECTION #4: "I need to think about it / Talk with my family"
YOUR RESPONSE: "Completely logical. This isn't a decision you make alone.

So here's what I suggest: Let's schedule another call for next week (maybe Tuesday or Thursday) when you have time to talk with [spouse/partner/advisor]. I'll prepare a summary with specific properties that fit your budget, and you both can review it together.

Does that work?"

Close & CTA (13-15 minutes)

YOU: "Okay, so here's what I'm going to do: 1) This week I'll send you a PDF with 5-7 properties that match exactly what you're looking for. 2) You review it, talk with your family if needed. 3) Next week we schedule another call (or video tour of the properties that interest you most). 4) If you decide to come to Mérida, we'll coordinate a 2-3 day trip to see the properties in person. Does this work for you? Tuesday or Thursday next week?" THEM: [They agree or ask more questions] YOU: "Perfect. I'm going to send everything today via WhatsApp and email. And we'll schedule a call for [DAY/TIME]."

🏠 Property Showing Flow (Post-Consultation)

Timeline: Consultation → 3-7 days → Property showing visit

Before the Showing

  • Send 3-5 property listings (via WhatsApp) that match their budget/type
  • Include: photos, price, location, key features, neighborhood overview
  • Offer virtual tour (Zoom walkthrough) if they want to pre-screen before visiting
  • Confirm: When they're coming to Mérida, how many days, budget again

During the Showing

✅ DO:
  • Show 4-6 properties in one day (not more, decision fatigue is real)
  • Walk through the neighborhood (let them feel it)
  • Point out nearby amenities: restaurants, banks, beaches, expat communities
  • Answer questions honestly (if a property isn't perfect, don't oversell)
  • Take notes on what they like/dislike
❌ DON'T:
  • Overwhelm them with too many properties (decision paralysis)
  • Pressure them to decide same day
  • Bad-mouth competitors or other neighborhoods
  • Make up features (be transparent about what needs renovation, etc.)
  • Ignore red flags (if title looks sketchy, tell them)

After the Showing

  • Same day: Send thank-you message. Ask: "Which properties interested you most?"
  • 24 hours: Send title reports for top 2-3 candidates
  • 48 hours: Follow up: "Any questions about the properties? Ready to move forward?"
  • Decision day: Once they pick one, start legal/title verification

✍️ Closing Process (Weeks 1-10 After Deal Agreement)

Timeline Your Action Buyer Expectation
Week 1-2 Title verification, legal review, fideicomiso setup Confirmation that property is clear, no liens
Week 3-4 Seller agreement finalized, all docs ready for notary Clear next steps, signature dates
Week 5-6 Buyer provides funds (via wire transfer), fideicomiso account opened Instructions for fund transfer, security assurance
Week 7-8 Notary signing (buyer can do remotely or in-person) Clear signing instructions, document copies
Week 9-10 Title recorded, fideicomiso registration complete Final ownership confirmation, keys/possession

Key Communication Points

💡 During closing, update buyer WEEKLY: Don't let them wonder what's happening. A simple message: "Hey! We're on track. Just waiting on [X]. Should have update by [DATE]." This reduces anxiety and builds trust.

🚩 Red Flags (When NOT to Proceed)

  • Unrealistic budget/timeline combination: "$50K property, close in 2 weeks" = not happening. Reset expectations or pass.
  • They won't commit to a showing: If they keep "just researching," they're not a buyer. Move on.
  • Financing issues surface mid-closing: If they suddenly can't produce funds, pause legal work immediately.
  • Title problems appear during verification: Liens, unclear ownership, squatters. Don't force the deal. Advise walking away.
  • They're comparing you to 10 other agents: You'll lose. Don't waste energy. Focus on committed leads.
