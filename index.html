FAQ

Questions we get before every audit

How is this different from running an SEO tool or a free audit myself?

SEO tools scan one channel. Our 360° audit covers 7 pillars simultaneously — website, SEO, social, email, CRM, paid media, and brand positioning — and scores them against each other. More importantly, we give you a revenue impact model: not just "your site speed is slow" but "this is costing you an estimated X leads per month." No tool does that.

What if I already work with a marketing agency?

Most of our clients have agencies. The audit gives you an independent, scored view of what's working and what isn't — so you can have informed conversations with your agency instead of taking their word for it. Several clients have used the report to hold their agencies accountable or renegotiate their contracts.

Do I need to give you access to my accounts?

For the Proof Sprint, no — we audit from the outside, the same way a prospect or competitor would see you. For the Full 360° Audit, we may request read-only access to Google Analytics, Search Console, or your CRM to get accurate traffic and conversion data. You control what you share, and we only need view permissions — never admin access.

What happens after I receive the report?

You get a 30-minute (Proof Sprint) or 60-minute (Full Audit) walkthrough call where we go through the findings together. After that, you can implement the fixes yourself — the report tells you exactly what to do and in what order. Most Full Audit clients choose to continue working with us on implementation: SEO content, automation builds, CRM setup, or ongoing monitoring. We scope that on the call based on what your audit actually found. There's no pressure — you decide what's right for your situation.

Is the $497 Proof Sprint worth it if I'm a small business?

The Proof Sprint is designed exactly for that: one focused pillar, scored 0–100, with USD gap estimates and a personal Loom walkthrough — delivered in 24 hours. If even one finding converts a single additional client, it pays for itself many times over. And if you decide to go all-in with the Full Audit within 30 days, the $497 is fully credited — you only pay the $2,000 difference.

How long does the Full 360° Audit take?

48 hours from receipt of your completed pre-audit questionnaire. You'll receive a scored PDF report, a prioritised action roadmap, and a revenue impact model. We then schedule your 60-minute strategy call within the following 48 hours.
