Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 19, 2026

Bernius Consulting ("we", "us") operates berniusconsulting.com. This policy explains what data we collect, why, and what you can do about it.

What we collect

  • Analytics data — pages visited, device and browser type, approximate location, collected via Google Analytics (anonymised where possible).
  • Contact details you give us — name, email, and anything you share when you book a call (Calendly), fill in a form (Tally), email us, or message us on WhatsApp.
  • Payment information — processed directly by Stripe or PayPal; we never see or store your card details.

How we use it

  • To respond to enquiries and deliver audits, reports and services you ordered.
  • To understand how the site is used and improve it.
  • To meet legal, tax and accounting obligations.

We do not sell your data, and we do not share it with third parties for their own marketing.

Cookies

We use cookies for site analytics and functionality. On your first visit you can accept or decline via the cookie banner; your choice is stored in your browser's local storage. You can clear it anytime in your browser settings.

Third-party services

  • Google Analytics — site usage statistics (Google LLC).
  • Calendly — call scheduling.
  • Tally — forms and quizzes.
  • Stripe / PayPal — payment processing.
  • WhatsApp — messaging (Meta Platforms).

Each of these processes data under its own privacy policy.

Your rights

You may request access, correction or deletion of your personal data at any time. Contact us and we will respond within 30 days.

Contact
